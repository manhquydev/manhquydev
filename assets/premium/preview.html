Filter Effects (effects/filters.svg)

Available Filters:

  • glass-blur — Main glassmorphism effect (blur 12px, 70% opacity)
  • glass-blur-light — Softer glass effect (blur 8px, 50% opacity)
  • glass-blur-strong — Heavy glass effect (blur 20px, 85% opacity)
  • glow-primary — Primary color glow (blue)
  • glow-secondary — Secondary color glow (purple)
  • glow-accent — Accent color glow (orange)
  • glow-success — Success color glow (green)
  • drop-shadow — Card shadow effect
  • inner-shadow — Inset shadow effect
  • noise — Subtle grain texture

Gradients: Card gradients, primary gradients, accent gradients for both light and dark modes
